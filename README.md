# Food Delivery Business Intelligence Dashboard

End-to-end Power BI project transforming raw food delivery data into actionable business insights across revenue, customer behavior, and delivery operations.

---

## Business Context

Food delivery platforms operate in a highly dynamic environment involving:
- Fluctuating customer demand  
- Delivery logistics complexity  
- Restaurant performance variability  

This project analyzes these factors to identify **operational inefficiencies and growth opportunities**.

---

## Key Business Questions

- Where are delivery delays impacting performance?
- How strong is customer retention?
- Which cities and restaurants underperform?
- What drives revenue concentration?
- How can operations be optimized?

---

##  Key Insights

###  Delivery Performance Issue
Nearly **48% of orders are delayed**, indicating major operational inefficiencies.

### Strong Customer Retention
Around **50% of users are repeat customers**, contributing significantly to total orders.

### City-Level Variability
Delivery time and cancellations vary across cities, highlighting geographic inefficiencies.

###  Revenue Concentration Risk
A small group of restaurants drives a large portion of revenue.

### Stable Business Trends
Order and revenue trends show consistent patterns over time.

---

##  Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Improve Delivery Efficiency
- Optimize delivery routes  
- Increase delivery capacity  

### 2. Strengthen Customer Retention
- Introduce loyalty programs  
- Target repeat users  

### 3. Optimize City Operations
- Focus on high-delay cities  
- Improve logistics planning  

### 4. Enhance Customer Experience
- Improve transparency  
- Reduce delivery delays  

### 5. Diversify Restaurant Contribution
- Promote mid-performing restaurants  
- Reduce dependency on top vendors
